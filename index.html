About the Magazine

BlueEcosystem serves as a global platform for researchers, students, industry professionals, farmers, policymakers and science communicators. The magazine publishes accessible, informative and impactful content that promotes sustainable aquatic and environmental development.

Scope of the Magazine

Aquaculture & Fisheries

Modern aquaculture technologies, sustainable fisheries and aquatic resource management.

Environment

Environmental sustainability, ecosystem conservation and climate resilience.

Agriculture

Innovations in agriculture, agribusiness and food security.

Food Processing

Emerging technologies and value-added food products.

Feed Nutrition

Animal nutrition, feed formulation and feed technology.

Aquatic Pollution

Pollution monitoring, remediation and environmental protection.

Marine Biology

Marine biodiversity, ecology and ocean sciences.

Animal Health

Disease management and health monitoring in aquatic and terrestrial systems.

Author Guidelines

Manuscript Preparation

  • Articles should be original and unpublished.
  • Word limit: 3-5 pages.
  • Font: Times New Roman, Size 12.
  • Line spacing: 1.5.
  • Figures and tables should be clearly numbered.
  • References should follow APA format.
  • Authors must provide full affiliation and email address.
  • Submitted articles will undergo editorial review.
  • AI generated contents are strictly prohibited.
