Author Guidelines

Manuscript Preparation

  • Manuscripts must be original and unpublished.
  • Language: English.
  • File format: MS Word (.doc/.docx).
  • Font: Times New Roman, Size 12.
  • Line spacing: 1.5.
  • Page size: A4 with 1-inch margins.
  • Page limit 2-5 pages

Manuscript Structure

  1. Title
  2. Authors and Affiliations
  3. Abstract (200–300 words)
  4. Keywords (3–6)
  5. Introduction
  6. Main body
  7. Conclusion
  8. References
  9. apa format for all references

Submission

Submit manuscripts through the journal website or email: blueecosystem2026@gmail.com

Publication Ethics

The journal follows ethical publishing standards. Plagiarism, data fabrication, and duplicate publication are strictly prohibited.
